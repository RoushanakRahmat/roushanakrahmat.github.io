About Me

I am an AI research scientist at Elekta, a world learning medical tech company. I am developing novel algorithms and models in deep learning to improve radiotherapy.


Prior to joining this industrial research, I was working at the Institute of Cancer Research, Cambridge Queens' College, University of Cambridge and St. Andrews University.


I obtained my PhD in Electrical, Electronics and Computer Engineering at Heriot-Watt University in collaboration with the University of Edinburgh.


My research interests are in the fields of deep learning, medical image analysis and computer vision. These structured models allow to model uncertainty and diversity, which enables novel applications of deep learning from personalization of products and therapies to decision making under uncertainty. I believe AI would revolutionize healthcare and personalize medical treatment in the near future.


In my free time I love running, yoga, swimming, paininting, reading books as well as empowering women in technology.
